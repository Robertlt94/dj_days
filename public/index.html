About DJ Days

A dynamic single-webpage application designed to bring music lovers and collectors a virtual DJ booth at their fingertips. Features allow users to see which album is currently playing, browse through a comprehensive list of albums, add new albums to their collection, and select any album to be the "now playing" music.
